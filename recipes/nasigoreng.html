Nasi Goreng


Description

Nasi goreng is a tradititonal dish that serves as a melting pot of Indonesia's history.

Ingridients:

  • Rice(preferably leftover rice)
  • Sweet soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • shallot
  • Chili
  • Oyster sauce
  • Sugar and Salt
  • Chives(optional)
  • Oil
  • Chicken

Steps:

  1. First, minced the garlics, shallots, and chives together until smooth.
  2. Then, add your oil to a pan and let it heat up for a little bit. After the oil heats up add the mince Ingridients (chopped garlics, shallots, and chives) to the pot. Stir them all up on a low heat until it smells nice.
  3. Add oysten sauce, soy sauce, and fish paste to the pot. Mix them well.
  4. After sauce is done, put it aside on a bowl or let it rest in the pan.
  5. Chop your chilis and seperate the seeds from the chili pots. Shred your chicken into bite-size pieces.
  6. Heat a little oil in a separate pan. Add the eggs and your shredded chicken to the pan, then scramble them until cooked.
  7. Add your rice, chili, chives, our pre-made sauce, sugar, sweet soy sauce to the scramble chicken eggs pan and cook them until ready.
  8. Nasi goreng is ready to be eaten up.
