Mashed Potatoes

Description

A dish that mashed potatoes

Ingridients:

  • 3 large russet potates, peeled and halved lengthwise
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingridients
  2. Place potatoes in a large pot and cover with cold salted water by about 1 inch. Bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover and simmer until tender and easily pierced with a fork, 20 to 25 minutes.
  3. Drain potatoes, then return to the pot. Turn the heat to high and allow potatoes to dry for about 30 seconds. Turn off heat.
  4. Mash potatoes with a potato masher twice around the pot, then mash in milk, and butter until smooth and fluffy.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper; whisk until evenly distributed, about 15 seconds.
  6. Mashed potatoes is ready to be eaten.
